# Vannevar Bush

American engineer, inventor, and science administrator. Director of the Office of Scientific Research and Development during World War II.

## Key Contribution

In his 1945 essay "As We May Think," Bush described the [[memex|Memex]] — a hypothetical device for personal knowledge storage with associative trails between documents. The essay is widely considered a visionary precursor to hypertext, the web, and personal knowledge management.

## Connection to LLM Knowledge Bases

[[andrej-karpathy|Andrej Karpathy]] cites Bush's Memex as a spiritual ancestor of the [[llm-knowledge-bases|LLM Knowledge Bases]] pattern. Both share the vision of a private, curated knowledge store where connections between documents are as valuable as the documents themselves. The critical difference: Bush's vision required human maintenance of all associative trails, while LLMs can handle that automatically.
